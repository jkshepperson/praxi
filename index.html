prepping patients for what's next

Automated engagement... with a human touch

Praxi sends automated and personalized reminders to patients scheduled for exams or procedures that require of prep work or NPO direction, before-hand. By utilizing BJ Fogg's Behavioral Model, Praxi ensures your patients are prepped and ready to go for all imaging and surgical procedures.
